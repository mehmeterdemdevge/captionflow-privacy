Privacy Policy

For CaptionFlow
Effective date: 2026-02-23

Summary: CaptionFlow does not collect personal information like your name, email, contacts, photos, or precise location. We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions and confirm whether Pro access is active.

Information We Collect

We do not ask you to create an account and we do not collect personal information such as:

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Contacts, address book, calendar
  • Photos, camera roll, or files
  • Precise location

Subscriptions (RevenueCat)

If you purchase a subscription, Apple processes the payment. We use RevenueCat to:

  • Validate subscription status (e.g., whether Pro is active)
  • Restore purchases on request
  • Enable Pro features in the app

For this functionality, RevenueCat may process:

  • Purchase information (e.g., subscription product, receipt/purchase history)
  • An app-scoped user identifier used to link subscription status to your device/app instance
  • Basic technical information needed for app functionality (e.g., locale/currency provided by the store)

We do not use RevenueCat data for advertising, and we do not sell your data.

Analytics & Tracking

CaptionFlow does not use advertising identifiers (IDFA) and does not track you across apps or websites for advertising purposes.

Third-Party Services

We use RevenueCat for subscription management. You can review their privacy policy here:

https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy/

Data Retention

We do not maintain our own user database. Subscription-related records are handled by Apple and RevenueCat as necessary to provide subscription functionality.

Children’s Privacy

CaptionFlow is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Choices

  • You can manage or cancel subscriptions in your Apple ID settings.
  • You can restore purchases from within the app’s settings.
